Frequently Asked Procurement Questions

What is your absolute minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We accept small trial sample volumes starting from just 1 Master Carton (AA Type box). We specialize in custom-tailored variety mixes inside a single box configuration to maximize buyer stock rotation.

Do you manage air freight allocations, or work with our agents?

We support both options. Under FOB terms, we transfer the secure cold cargo right to your appointed airline carrier agent at JKIA Nairobi. Under CIF terms, we leverage our 15+ years of air carrier relations to secure space and build freight costs into your final structural invoice.
